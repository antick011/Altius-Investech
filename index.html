How It Works

Follow these steps to complete your transactions smoothly.

Step 1 - SignUp and complete KYC

Signup on our platform, complete your KYC to verify your account with us.

Step 3 - Complete Payment

Select a payment method and complete the payment for your order.

Step 2 - Place order

Select a company and the number of shares you would like to purchase and place an order.

Step 4 - Trade gets executed under 24 hours

Once we receive your payment, the shares are transferred to your DEMAT account within 24 hours.

For Buyer and Seller

How buyers and sellers can make the most of our platform.

For Buyer

1. Signup on our trade platform and complete your KYC with us

2. Explore from the list of companies to invest in

3. Do your research and place a buy order for the quantity you wish to buy

4. Select a mode of payment and complete your payment either online or offline

5. Once the payment is made, you will receive your shares in your DEMAT within 24 hours

For Seller

1. Signup on our trade platform and complete your KYC with us

2. Select the company you want to liquidate, enter the quantity and rate per share you want to place your order at

3. Initiate transfer of shares

4. Receive funds within 24 hours of receipt of shares.
